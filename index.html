Maddie & Ward

September 11, 2021
Richmond, Virginia
Back where it all began

Maddie and Ward met in Richmond, VA, in 2015 at a Friendsgiving party. Ward was wearing a Star Wars shirt and offered Maddie some apple cider, which she declined. Although, she (oddly) accepted his invitation a few days later to what would be their first date, his company’s Christmas party. From then to now, they spent some time dating long distance between Richmond & New York City, met in the middle for a few years in Washington, DC, trekked in Nepal, bought a dog and named him Chessy, and more recently, became homeowners in Richmond, where they can’t wait to celebrate their wedding with those that mean most to them!
