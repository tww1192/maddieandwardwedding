Back where it all began

Maddie and Ward met in Richmond, VA in 2015 at a friends-giving party. Ward was wearing a Star Wars shirt and offered Maddie some apple cider - which she declined. Fast forward some a few dates, they found themselves long distance between Washington D.C. and New York. After years of long distance they agreed to meet in D.C.
